About The British University In Dubai

The British University in Dubai (BUiD) stands as a beacon of academic excellence and innovation in the heart of the Middle East. Established in 2004, BUiD holds the distinction of being the Middle East's first research-based, postgraduate university. Rooted in the rich traditions of British higher education, BUiD offers a diverse array of cutting-edge programs tailored to meet the evolving needs of the region. With a commitment to fostering a dynamic learning environment, BUiD provides students with unparalleled opportunities to engage in interdisciplinary research, collaborate with esteemed faculty, and cultivate critical thinking skills necessary for success in today's global landscape. Embracing diversity and inclusivity, BUiD serves as a vibrant hub for intellectual discourse, cultural exchange, and transformative learning experiences, empowering its graduates to become leaders and innovators in their respective fields. Through its unwavering dedication to excellence and forward-thinking approach, BUiD continues to shape the future of education in the UAE and beyond.

About Us

Our team of five students Abdullah Albalooshi , Abdullah Mansour , Khalid bin Thabit , Meshal Alrajaby , Yousef Alrantisi, with support from our university, worked together on a special project: making a website for a good cause. We all really care about using technology to help people, so we combined our different skills to create a website that we hope will make our community better. We focused on being kind and working together, paying close attention to every part of the website, from how it looks to how it works. We put a lot of effort into making it easy to use and helpful for those who need it. We're proud to have been part of this project and excited about the positive impact our website can have on people's lives.
